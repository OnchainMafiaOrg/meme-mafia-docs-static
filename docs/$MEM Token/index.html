Technical Description: The $MEM token ecosystem incorporates a strategic transaction fee mechanism, applying a 1% fee to each transaction. This fee serves a tri-fold purpose: enhancing holder value, supporting liquidity, and ensuring a continuously deflationary supply.

  • Redistribution: 0.5% of transaction fees are returned to $MEM holders, rewarding those who invest in and hold onto the token.
  • Liquidity Provision: 0.45% contributes to the liquidity pools, vital for maintaining a stable and fluid market.
  • Buyback and Burn: The remaining 0.05% is allocated to buy back and burn $MEM tokens, effectively reducing the total supply and increasing scarcity.

Features:

  • Smart Contract Automation: Utilizes smart contracts for efficient and transparent fee collection and distribution.
  • Burn Function: A dedicated mechanism to permanently remove a portion of $MEM from circulation, reinforcing its deflationary nature.
  • Visibility and Verification: Each transaction and burn event is recorded on the blockchain, offering real-time insight into the token's circulating supply and deflationary actions.
